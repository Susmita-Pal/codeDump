Greetings from Women in Open Source chapter,

We are ecstatic to announce that out of a whopping number of registrants, you have been shortlisted for the next and the final round- The Telephonic Interview for the Women in Open Source's Executive Board.
Congratulations!

So, what is the next round?
We'll be holding a short interview session in order to interact with you.The interview lasts for 10 minutes based on your interest in the department to confirm whether you'd be a good fit for the position as well as the chapter in an entirety.

You will be interviewed for the position based on the field you've chosen. Be prepared in case you would like to show us any previous work of yours.

Medium of interaction
You need to join the Discord channel of WiOS where you'll be provided with your roles.

Keep track of your email as further instructions on the interview and the link to join Discord will be communicated to you shortly.

Are there any rules that you should keep in mind?
A few key points to note if you are planning on proceeding ahead with the Women in Open Source chapter.

You will be required to select the Women in Open Source chapter as your core club during the club registration process. You cannot simultaneously be part of the Executive Board of two different technical clubs. Being a part of a chapter would require a commitment to dedicating your time and effort towards the growth of the club, and as such, you will be required to perform your duties as and when required.

Further details regarding the slot and which date you will be required to be present will be notified in a follow up email.

Respond to this email with your confirmation and with your discord tag, if you would like to participate in the EB selection process.
